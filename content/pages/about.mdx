## Our story

All Pets Veterinary Hospital was founded on a simple idea: that great veterinary
medicine and genuine warmth belong together. We're a locally rooted practice in
Norman, Oklahoma, caring for the dogs, cats, and families of our community.

## How we care

From routine wellness visits to dental care and surgery, we take the time to
explain what's happening and why — no rushed appointments, no jargon. We treat
every patient like our own.
