A wellness exam is the foundation of preventive care. Our veterinarians perform a
thorough head-to-tail evaluation — heart, lungs, eyes, ears, skin, joints, and
teeth — to spot small issues before they become big ones.

We'll talk through nutrition, weight, behavior, and a vaccination and
parasite-prevention plan tailored to your pet's age and lifestyle. Most healthy
pets benefit from an exam at least once a year; seniors and puppies/kittens more
often.
